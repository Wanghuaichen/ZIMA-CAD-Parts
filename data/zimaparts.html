Welcome to ZIMA-Parts!

ZIMA-Parts is simple application for searching and downloading CAD parts from our server. Parts are primarily ordered by companies who creates or deliver these parts, or by national standards.

This application is released under GNU/GPL license but service itself is paid.

ZIMA-Parts is written in C++ using Qt framework. It's multiplatform - runs on Windows, Mac and Linux.
