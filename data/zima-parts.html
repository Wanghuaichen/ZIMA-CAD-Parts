ZIMA-Parts is a simple application for searching and downloading CAD parts from our server. Parts are primarily ordered by companies who creates or deliver these parts, or by national standards.

This application was created by ZIMA-Construction and it is released under GNU/GPLv3 license but service itself is paid.

ZIMA-Parts is written in C++ using Qt framework. It's multiplatform - runs on Windows, Mac and Linux.
